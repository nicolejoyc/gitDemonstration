My Top 3 Elevator Pitches

  • Gift Bag

    For planners
    Who need a way to organize and remember gifts throughout the year
    The Gift Bag
    Is a gift organizing tool
    That helps prevent forgetting what to get family or friends when the time comes around
    Unlike a typical notes app
    Our product is focused only on gift ideas, making it easier to remember, and more convenient to store and organize them

  • Collecting America

    For quarter collectors
    Who need a way to keep track of their collection
    The Collecting America
    Is a quarter collection tool
    That allows collectors to keep specific details about the coins in their collection
    Unlike using a physical coin map
    Our product is virtual, as well as dynamic, so you can add coins that might not have a place on a map

  • Paw Prints

    For anyone in a community with pets
    Who needs to find or report a lost pet
    The Paw Prints
    Is a pet locater tool
    That allows convenient communication between finders and seekers
    Unlike Facebook or other social media to ask for help
    Our product allows not only close friends to be able to help search, but rather anyone in the community
